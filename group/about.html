About

Welcome to Friends of the Alpine, your premier travel agency for unforgettable trips to the stunning Alps. We offer a range of guided tours, hiking expeditions, and skiing adventures for all experience levels. Our team of knowledgeable guides and local experts is dedicated to providing you with an unforgettable trip to one of the world's most breathtaking destinations.

Our mission

Sustainable, unforgettable travel to the Alps

History

Friends of the Alpine was founded in 2005 by a group of passionate mountaineers who wanted to share their love of the Alps with others. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the leading travel agencies for trips to the region, earning numerous awards and accolades along the way. Today, our team of experienced guides and travel experts is dedicated to providing our customers with the trip of a lifetime, while also promoting sustainable tourism practices that help to protect the natural beauty of the Alps.
