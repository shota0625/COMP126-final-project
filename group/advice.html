Travel Advice

Food

To save money while traveling, be sure to visit one of the many market stalls in villages for fresh, cheap produce. You'll be supporting local farmers and eating well!

Lodging

To save money on Lodging avoid AirBNB and Hotels. Instead, make sure to utilize hostels, and couch surfing *a cheap way to see the country and a great way to make new friends*

Activities

Be sure to take advantage of your surroundings. Either learn from one of many ski, schools or if you already know how to ski there are sure to be many ski areas to fit your liking.
